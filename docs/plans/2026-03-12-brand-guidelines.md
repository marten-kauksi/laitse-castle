# Laitse Loss — Brand Guidelines

**Date:** 2026-03-12
**Status:** Approved

## Brand Essence

Laitse Loss is a historic neo-Gothic castle venue near Tallinn. The brand communicates **warm, bookish luxury** — sophisticated but inviting, historic but alive.

**Not** cold museum. **Not** generic hotel. **Not** fairy-tale kitsch.

## Color Palette

### Primary

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Warm White | `#FAF7F2` | Page backgrounds, primary surface |
| Burgundy | `#8B1A1A` | Primary accent, CTAs, headlines accent, links |
| Burgundy Hover | `#6B1414` | Interactive states |

### Secondary

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Gold | `#B8860B` | Decorative accents, flourishes, rules |
| Warm Dark | `#2C2420` | Headings, primary text |
| Stone | `#7A6F64` | Secondary text, captions, meta info |

### Gold Gradient

Used for decorative flourishes, horizontal rules, and accent lines.

```
Horizontal: #d19e1d → #ffd86e → #e3a812
Vertical: same stops, 180deg
```

### Usage Rules

- **White is primary.** Pages should feel spacious and light.
- **Burgundy is the action color.** Buttons, links, key accents.
- **Gold is decorative only.** Never used for text or interactive elements.
- Red + gold together is the brand differentiator — always make them prominent, not subtle.

## Typography

### Font Stack

| Role | Font | Weight | Usage |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| Display / H1 | Fraunces | 200 | Page titles, hero headings. Optical sizing enabled. |
| Subtitle / H2-H3 | Cormorant Infant | 300 italic, 400, 500 | Section headings, subtitles, pull-quotes |
| Body | Libre Baskerville | 400, 400 italic, 700 | Paragraph text, long-form content |
| UI / Small | Lato | 300, 400, 700 | Navigation, buttons, labels, meta text |

### Typography Rules

- Headings use generous letter-spacing (`0.04em` for Fraunces)
- Subtitles in Cormorant Infant italic carry the "bookish" feel
- Body text in Libre Baskerville at comfortable reading size (16-17px)
- UI elements in Lato for clarity and contrast with serif content
- All-caps sparingly — only for small labels and navigation (use Lato or Cormorant Infant weight 500 with wide tracking)

## Decorative Elements

### Flourishes

Two SVG flourishes used as section decorators:

- **fl-simple** — symmetrical ornamental design, used at larger sizes, gold gradient fill
- **fl-vintage** — flowing horizontal calligraphic line, used smaller, burgundy or faded gold fill

### Rules & Dividers

- **Gold rule** — 1px, gold gradient, used between heading and subtitle
- **Burgundy rule** — 2px, solid burgundy, used as section dividers
- **Gold accent** — 40px wide, 2px, gold gradient, used as subtle separators

### Side Lines

- Vertical gold gradient lines (1px, 180px tall) positioned at page edges for hero sections
- Adds a sense of framing without boxing content in

### Top Stripe

- 4px burgundy bar at top of hero sections
- 1px gold gradient line beneath it
- Signals brand identity immediately

## Buttons

### Primary (Filled)

- Background: burgundy `#8B1A1A`
- Text: warm-white `#FAF7F2`
- Font: varies (Outfit or Lato, weight 400-500)
- Letter-spacing: `0.18em`, uppercase
- Padding: `16px 40px`
- Hover: darker burgundy `#6B1414`

### Secondary (Outline)

- Border: 1.5px solid burgundy
- Text: burgundy
- Same font/spacing as primary
- Hover: fills with burgundy, text becomes white

## Tone of Voice

### Estonian (primary)

- Warm, dignified, inviting
- Not stiff or overly formal — like a gracious host, not a butler
- History is told with respect, not fairy-tale embellishment
- Service descriptions are clear and confident, not salesy

### English (secondary)

- Same tone, natural English (not translated-sounding)
- Keep Estonian place names and proper nouns

### Copy Rules

- Lead with what visitors care about, not what the castle wants to say
- Practical info (capacity, location, contact) should be easy to find
- No fairy-tale narratives on service pages — save storytelling for the history page
- All copy written from scratch — do NOT copy from previous owner's site (laitseloss.ee)

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do

- Use generous whitespace — let the typography breathe
- Lead with burgundy + gold accents on every page
- Keep photography warm-toned to match the palette
- Use flourishes sparingly as section markers, not decoration overload
- Make contact info visible on every page

### Don't

- Copy any design elements from laitseloss.ee (legal constraint)
- Use gold for interactive elements (buttons, links) — gold is decorative only
- Overcrowd pages with decorative elements
- Use cold/blue tones in photography
- Write fairy-tale style copy on service pages
- Use more than 2 flourishes per page
